CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT & GENERAL CONTRACTING

At AMEC, we deliver end to end Construction Management Solutions from Pre-Construction to Project Completion including budgeting, estimating, value engineering, project scheduling, logistics, and the coordination of sub-contractors, equipment, and materials. AMEC specializes in multi-family, mixed-used residential and retail projects that provide a community living atmosphere.


We take pride in our ability to deliver quality projects while meeting aggressive deadlines. We believe in teamwork and building strong relationships with our clients and trusted subcontractors.


DUMPSTERS & RECYCLING

AMEC has a proven track record of being the "Go To" contractor for large and complicated site/civil projects and demolition projects. Whether hiring AMEC directly for site/civil projects or demolition needs or hiring AMEC as the Construction Manager who will self perform these services, you can't go wrong choosing AMEC. Getting out of the ground is the make or break of a great project. With our capabilities, you never have to worry.


Additionally, AMEC's dumpster and recycling services offer you the ability to order open top, construction and demolition dumpsters with ease. We own and operate three recycling facilities across Connecticut and New York allowing for us to provide LEED Waste Reporting for you as part of your building's certification process.
